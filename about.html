The story

Why I built Tarumak Studio

After 15 years of freelance design work, I found myself repeatedly installing the same clunky software, hitting paywalls on simple tools, and explaining to clients why basic image compression required a monthly subscription.

So I built Tarumak Studio — a collection of tools I actually use every day. Image compressors, PDF editors, color palette generators, social media image resizers. Every single one runs entirely in your browser, with no uploads, no sign-ups, and no catches.

The name Tarumak is my personal brand — a reminder that the best tools are the ones that just work, without getting in the way.

I specialise in UI/UX design, brand identity, and marketing assets for clients in hospitality, finance, and consumer brands. Tarumak Studio is both my gift to the design community and my portfolio — a demonstration of what I can build for you.
