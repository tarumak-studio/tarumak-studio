Best Free Marketing Tools for Designers and Creators in 2026

June 2026 7 min read

Marketing tools used to mean expensive subscriptions. In 2026, every tool a designer or marketer needs for campaigns, social media, and content creation runs free in the browser. Here are the most useful ones.

Social media image tools

Social Media Image Resizer

Resize any image to exact platform dimensions for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest and WhatsApp. Drag-to-crop, multiple modes. The single most-used marketing tool for any designer.

Open Graph Image Generator

Create 1200×630 OG images for link previews on social media. Add title text, brand colours and a logo — download as PNG. Essential for content marketers and bloggers.

Campaign and analytics tools

UTM Link Builder

Build UTM-tagged campaign URLs for Google Analytics 4. Also decodes existing UTM links. Absolutely essential for tracking what actually drives website traffic.

Ad Copy Generator

Generate headlines, body copy and CTAs for Facebook, Google and Instagram ads. Template-based — no AI subscription needed.

Brand and design tools

Color Palette Generator

Extract color palettes from images or generate harmonious color schemes. Ideal for maintaining brand consistency across campaigns.

Brand Color Extractor

Upload a client's logo and extract the exact brand colors as HEX, RGB, HSL and CSS variables in seconds.

CTA Button Generator

Design call-to-action buttons with live preview and download as PNG or copy the CSS. Great for landing page prototypes and client presentations.

Lead generation tools

PDF Lead Magnet Creator

Build professional PDF checklists, guides and cheatsheets to grow an email list. Choose template style, add brand colours, download instantly.

What is the most important free marketing tool?

For most marketing designers, the Social Media Image Resizer and UTM Builder provide the most daily value. Image resizing is needed for every campaign; UTM tracking shows what actually works.
