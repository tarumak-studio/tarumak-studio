Free Online Tools Every Freelancer Needs in 2026

June 2026 6 min read

Freelancing means wearing every hat. Designer, marketer, accountant, and customer support — all in one day. The right free tools save hours every week. Here are the ones worth bookmarking.

Client-facing design tools

Image compression (Image Compressor)

Compress images before sending deliverables by email or uploading to client portals. A 50MB folder of images becomes 5MB without visible quality loss.

Watermarking (Watermark Tool)

Always watermark preview images before client approval. Protects work until payment is received.

PDF merger (PDF Merger)

Combine deliverables, proposals, and contracts into single PDFs for professional client delivery.

Marketing your freelance business

Social media content (Social Image Resizer)

Resize portfolio pieces for every platform without manual cropping in Photoshop.

Campaign tracking (UTM Builder)

If you are running any ads or sharing links to your portfolio, UTM parameters tell you exactly which sources drive enquiries.

Lead magnets (PDF Lead Magnet Creator)

Create a free checklist or guide as an email capture tool. "10 questions to ask before hiring a designer" type resources work extremely well.

Brand consistency tools

Color extraction (Brand Color Extractor)

Upload a client's logo at the start of every project and extract their exact brand colors as CSS variables. Takes 10 seconds, saves hours of guessing.

All tools on Tarumak Studio are completely free, browser-based, and private. Your files never leave your device — essential when handling client confidential materials.

What is the best free tool for freelance designers?

The Image Compressor and PDF Merger solve the most common daily problems. The Brand Color Extractor and Social Image Resizer are close runners-up for client work efficiency.
