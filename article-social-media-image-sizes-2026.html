Social Media Image Sizes 2026 — Complete Cheatsheet

June 2026 4 min read

Why image sizes matter

Each platform has its own optimal image dimensions. Use the wrong size and your image gets cropped awkwardly, appears blurry, or gets stretched — all of which look unprofessional. The Social Media Image Resizer handles all platforms automatically.

Resize Images for Social Media →

Instagram image sizes 2026

  • Square Post: 1080 × 1080 px (1:1)
  • Portrait Post: 1080 × 1350 px (4:5) — gets more screen real estate
  • Landscape Post: 1080 × 566 px (1.91:1)
  • Story / Reel: 1080 × 1920 px (9:16)
  • Profile Picture: 320 × 320 px (displays at 110px)

Facebook image sizes 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 630 px
  • Cover Photo: 820 × 312 px
  • Story: 1080 × 1920 px
  • Profile Picture: 170 × 170 px

Twitter / X image sizes 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 675 px (16:9)
  • Header / Banner: 1500 × 500 px
  • Profile Picture: 400 × 400 px (displays as circle)

LinkedIn image sizes 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 628 px
  • Personal Banner: 1584 × 396 px
  • Company Logo: 300 × 300 px
  • Company Banner: 1128 × 191 px

YouTube image sizes 2026

  • Thumbnail: 1280 × 720 px (16:9) — minimum 640px wide
  • Channel Art: 2560 × 1440 px
  • Profile Picture: 800 × 800 px

Pinterest image sizes 2026

  • Standard Pin: 1000 × 1500 px (2:3) — performs best
  • Square Pin: 1000 × 1000 px
  • Story Pin: 1080 × 1920 px

Resize any image to any of these sizes instantly with the free Social Media Image Resizer. Just upload, pick a platform, drag to crop, and download.

What resolution should social media images be?

Always export at 72 DPI for digital use — higher DPI does not improve quality on screens and only increases file size. The pixel dimensions listed above are what matters.

What file format should I use for social media?

JPG for photographs (smaller file size). PNG for graphics with text or transparency. Most platforms convert images anyway, so quality difference is minimal.
