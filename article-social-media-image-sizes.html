Social Media Image Sizes 2026 — Complete Cheatsheet

2026-06-01 4 min read Marketing Designer

Use the wrong image size and your content gets cropped, blurred or stretched. The Social Media Image Resizer handles all platforms automatically — pick a platform, upload, crop, download.

Resize for Social Media Free →

Instagram 2026

  • Square Post: 1080 × 1080 px (1:1)
  • Portrait Post: 1080 × 1350 px (4:5) — gets more feed space
  • Story / Reel: 1080 × 1920 px (9:16)

Facebook 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 630 px
  • Cover Photo: 820 × 312 px
  • Story: 1080 × 1920 px

Twitter / X 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 675 px (16:9)
  • Header / Banner: 1500 × 500 px

LinkedIn 2026

  • Post Image: 1200 × 628 px
  • Personal Banner: 1584 × 396 px

YouTube 2026

  • Thumbnail: 1280 × 720 px (16:9)
  • Channel Art: 2560 × 1440 px

Pinterest 2026

  • Standard Pin: 1000 × 1500 px (2:3) — performs best
  • Story Pin: 1080 × 1920 px
What resolution should social media images be?

Export at 72 DPI for digital — higher DPI does not improve quality on screens. The pixel dimensions above are what matters.
