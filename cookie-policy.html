Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites. They are used to remember preferences, analyse traffic, and enable certain functionality. Tarumak Studio uses a minimal set of storage technologies to provide a good experience.

What we use

1. localStorage (essential)

We use browser localStorage — not cookies — to store:

  • Theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Recently used tools so you can quickly return to them
  • Usage counter stored locally on your device only
  • Cookie consent preference so we do not ask you again

localStorage data never leaves your device and cannot be accessed by us or any third party. You can clear it at any time in your browser settings.

2. Google Analytics (analytical)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 which sets cookies to track aggregate usage statistics. These cookies are:

  • _ga: Distinguishes users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_*: Maintains session state. Expires after 2 years.

These cookies do not identify you personally. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

3. Google AdSense (advertising, if applicable)

If you have consented to advertising cookies, we may display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can manage Google ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality. Visit allaboutcookies.org for guidance on managing cookies in your browser.
