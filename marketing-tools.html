Frequently Asked Questions

What social media platforms does the image resizer support?

Instagram (post, story, reel thumbnail, profile), Facebook (post, cover, story), Twitter/X (post, header), LinkedIn (post, company cover, profile), YouTube (thumbnail, channel art), Pinterest (pin, board cover), and TikTok (profile, video thumbnail). All dimensions are updated for 2026 platform specifications.

What are UTM parameters and why do I need them?

UTM parameters are tracking tags added to URLs that tell Google Analytics where a visitor came from. Without them, traffic from email campaigns, social posts, and paid ads all appears as Direct in your analytics. With UTM parameters you can see exactly which campaign and channel drove each visit. The UTM Builder formats the URL correctly and URL-encodes all parameter values automatically.

Can I use my own logo in the Open Graph image generator?

Yes. The OG Image Generator includes a logo upload option — your logo is processed in the browser and composited onto the 1200×630 canvas locally. The output PNG is ready to use in the og:image meta tag of any web page.

How many colours does the palette generator extract?

Up to 8 dominant colours from any uploaded image. The algorithm clusters pixel colours and identifies the most visually significant hues, excluding near-white and near-black backgrounds by default. Results show HEX, RGB, and HSL values with a one-click copy button for each.

Does the CTA Button Generator output work in email clients?

The generator outputs inline-styled HTML table-based buttons that are compatible with all major email clients including Outlook, Gmail, and Apple Mail. The live preview shows desktop and mobile appearance. For web landing pages, the CSS class-based output is also available.
