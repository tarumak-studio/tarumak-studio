Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

The short version

Tarumak Studio does not collect, store or transmit your personal data or your files. Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

What we do not collect

  • No account registration — we have no user accounts
  • No file uploads — all processing happens locally in your browser
  • No personal information — name, email, address or phone number
  • No payment information
  • No IP address logging on our servers

localStorage (browser storage)

We use your browser's localStorage to remember your theme preference (light/dark), recently used tools, a local usage counter, and your cookie consent preference. This data is stored only in your browser and cannot be accessed by us.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage — which tools are most popular and where visitors come from. It does not receive your files. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

Affiliate links

Some pages contain affiliate links. If you purchase through one, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
