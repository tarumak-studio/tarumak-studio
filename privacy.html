Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

The short version

Tarumak Studio does not collect, store or transmit your personal data or your files. Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

What we do not collect

  • No account registration — we have no user accounts
  • No file uploads — all processing happens locally in your browser
  • No personal information — name, email, address, phone number
  • No payment information
  • No IP address logging on our servers

localStorage (browser storage)

We use your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Theme preference — light or dark mode
  • Recently used tools — so you can quickly return to tools you use often
  • Usage counter — a local count of how many times you have used tools (stored only on your device)
  • Cookie consent preference — whether you accepted or declined

This data is stored only in your browser. We cannot access it. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser storage or using your browser's privacy tools.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate — for example, which tools are most popular and where visitors come from. Google Analytics does not receive your files or any tool input. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-party CDN libraries

Some tools use open-source libraries loaded from third-party CDNs (pdf-lib, PDF.js, jsPDF, QRCode.js). These CDNs may log your IP address for their own purposes. The libraries themselves do not receive your file content.

Affiliate links

Some pages contain affiliate links to third-party services (such as Canva, Namecheap, and ConvertKit). If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we believe are useful.

Children's privacy

Tarumak Studio does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will note the date of the last update at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@tarumakstudio.com.
