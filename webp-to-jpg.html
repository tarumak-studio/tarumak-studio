Frequently asked questions

About WebP to JPG Converter and how Tarumak Studio works.

Why convert WebP to JPG?

Some older software, social media platforms and email clients do not support WebP. Converting to JPG ensures the image displays everywhere.

Does the conversion reduce image quality?

Slightly, since JPG uses lossy compression. Setting quality to 85–90% keeps the visual result close to the original WebP.

Can I batch-convert multiple WebP files?

Yes — drop multiple WebP images at once and download the converted JPGs together as a ZIP.

Is WebP to JPG Converter really free?

Yes — completely free, with no usage limits, no watermarks and no premium wall. Every tool on Tarumak Studio is free to use.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There is no sign-up, no login and no email required. Open the tool and use it immediately.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. WebP to JPG Converter runs entirely in your browser — your files are processed locally and never uploaded, which also makes it faster than server-based alternatives.

What formats does it support?

WebP to JPG Converter works with WEBP→JPG.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The tool runs in any modern browser on desktop, tablet or phone — no app install needed.
